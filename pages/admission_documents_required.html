Documents Required

Documents Required For UG Admission (First Year Engineering)
  1. DTE Final Allotment letter
  2. ARC Confirmation letter
  3. MHT-CET 2020/ JEE(Main) 2020 score card (Print)
  4. SSC mark sheet
  5. HSC mark sheet
  6. Leaving / Transfer certificate
  7. Migration certificate (For CBSE/JEE students)
  8. Nationality certificate
  9. Domicile Certificate
  10. Affidavit if Year gap
  11. Caste certificate
  12. Non-creamy layer certificate (except SC/ST)
  13. Caste validity certificate
  14. Original Pro-forma-H (If caste validity not received)
  15. Defense certificate (I/II/III) (For Ex-servicemen/Active domicile/Active non-domicile)
  16. J & K Migrant certificate (For J & K Candidates)
  17. Adhar Card Xerox
  18. Income Certificate Xerox (if less than 8 Lac)
  19. Two colour Photographs

Note: All original & one attested Xerox copy of following documents
Note: S. No. 11 to 14 for Reserved Category students only
